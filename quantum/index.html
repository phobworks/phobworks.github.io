Integrated Services Coordination

In our commitment to efficiency and cost-effectiveness, we offer integrated services coordination, streamlining communication and operations between rig and office. This approach minimizes NonProductive Time (NPT) and enhances overall project efficiency. Our Project Management Group provides a single point of contact to manage all QUANTUM MARINE LIMITED and third-party providers before and during drilling operations.

Elements of Service Include:

Builds location

Coordinates QUANTUM MARINE LIMITED and third-party services

Coordinates logistics

Creates cargo manifests, daily reports, and other documents

On-site survey

Equipment inspection, servicing, and scheduling

Reduction of Non-Productive Time (NPT)

Improvement in Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) compliance

Extensive offshore, deep-water, and remote project experience

QUANTUM MARINE LIMITED embodies enduring strength and a pioneering spirit, continuously seeking innovative and efficient ways to build. Our century-long experience in construction has integrated technological advancements, such as automated material expediting, electronic safety tracking, online collaboration tools, and Building Information Modeling (BIM), into our practices to deliver better projects more efficiently. While embracing modern technologies, our values remain rooted in trust and honorable actions, earning us the role of a trusted building advisor to our clients.

Pre-Construction:

Regardless of the delivery method or combination of services chosen, we consistently build excellence and trust. Our BuildersDriven Pre-Constructionism services offer insights into design options' value, helping clients make informed choices, eliminate cost surprises, accelerate facility turnover, and optimize building programs within budget constraints.
