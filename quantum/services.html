At QUANTUM MARINE LIMITED, our strength lies in offering a comprehensive range of start-to-finish services, encompassing initial planning, design, construction, start-up, operations, and maintenance. From the pre-feasibility phase through conceptual design to detailed design and implementation, we are committed to developing projects with precision and excellence.

Our services extend across diverse onshore and offshore activities, covering the following broad areas:



Total Project Capability

We provide end-to-end project management, ensuring a seamless transition from planning to execution, encompassing all facets of a project's lifecycle.

Integrated Engineering Services and Consultancy

Our expertise includes integrated engineering services, consultancy, equipment procurement, fabrication, and installation to meet the varied needs of our clients.

Pipelines/Flow lines Construction, Wellhead Maintenance, and Repairs

We specialize in constructing pipelines and flow lines, along with comprehensive wellhead maintenance and repair services.

Production Facilities - New Build, Upgrade, Operations, and Maintenance

Our proficiency spans the design, construction, and ongoing maintenance of production facilities, whether establishing new builds, upgrades, or managing day-to-day operations.

Environmental Protection and Waste Management Services

We are dedicated to environmental stewardship, offering services that focus on protection and responsible management of the environment, including waste management solutions.

QUANTUM MARINE LIMITED is rapidly evolving under the guidance of competent and prudent leadership, establishing itself as a renowned name synonymous with quality. Our operational experiences in the environmentally sensitive Niger Delta have been marked by an exemplary health, safety, and environment management policy, resulting in a track record devoid of any loss of life or time due to accidents since our inception.

Corporate Services

Our corporate services, in collaboration with our partners, span six different yet interrelated areas:


Deep Water Oil Well Drilling Services, Wellhead Maintenance, Platform Cleanup, and Remediation.

Oil & Gas Services - Engineering, Procurement, Installation, and Construction, Pipeline/Flow Construction, etc.

Marine and Logistic Support Services - Marine Equipment Leasing, Construction, Sales and Maintenance/Construction Support Management, and Solutions

Dredging and Reclamation Works.

Offshore Vessel Chartering.

Road Transportation and Logistics.
