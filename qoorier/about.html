About us

Qoorier Limited was incorporated in Nigeria with RC. No: 200192 on July 7th, 1999 as a privately owned Limited Liability Company and has since been involved in full fledge logistic services in all sector of the economy.

With a talented and dedicated group of professionals, our diverse expertise enables us to implement a wide range of logistic activities from conception to completion.

Our Mission

To be a profitable key player in the Nigerian built environment by providing quality world class logistic services

Our Value

Strong values inspire good and ethical practices. At Qoorier Limited our processes, staﬀ, interns and partners who work with us all subscribe to the core values of services, integrity, excellence, value of money and eﬃciency.

Resource

Qoorier Limited is dedicated to providing high quality products and services covering the full range of operations in the industry. Qoorier considerable resources allow for deliveries to be completed on time to a high standard while minimizing costs and expenses. Qoorier is able to do this because of:

  • A workforce of highly trained Nigerian Drivers and professionals.
  • A fleet of vehicles and motorcycles fully owned and well maintained.

These advantages help Qoorier to be the first choice for any delivery. They help save time and money.
