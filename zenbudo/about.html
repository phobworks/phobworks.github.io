About Us

We are a Marital Arts Academy committed to the promotion of peace and character building through the practice of traditional karate and meditaion.

At no other time has there been a strong call as there is today, for us to reawaken those traditional values, which have stood the test of time. And there i no better place to start this process than within ourselves. it is through knowledge of self that we can fully begin to manifest our four dimensions of intelligence (Body), Mental intelligence (Mind), Emotional intelligence (Heart) and Spiritual intelligence (Soul). We begin this process for you at Zen Budo Karate through our commitment to Self Defense, Fitness, Discipline and Meditation.

Vision

To be the service provider of choice for private individuals, schools, Corporate and Governmental Institutions in our market place.

Mission

To promote peace and enhance character building through the practice and teaching of traditional karate and meditation.

Sensei Amakiri Oruamabo

MD/Chief Instructor

(4th Dan)

Is a member of both the Nigerian Karate Association (NKA) and the Zensyokan Karate Organization (Japan). He started his karate studies in 1988 under the tutelage of Mezie-Okoye Sensei, the founder of All-Stars Shotokan Karate Association. Having started karate at a young age, Sensei Oruamabo has a firsthand experience on the developmental impact of karate. His consistent training over years has also seen him derive the health and mental benefits of the art.

A licensed Zen Instructor, Sensei Oruamabo believes that karate isn’t just about kicking and punching but a necessary tool through which we can develop and prepare the individual to approach life’s challenges with unwavering self-confidence and self-belief.

As the MD/Chief Instructor, Sensei Oruamabo is responsible for both business development and developing a training program that is in line with the company’s corporate vision.

Sensei Sensei Saidi Bakare

Technical Director

(4th Dan)

A versatile and accomplished karate-ka, Sensei Bakare heads the Zen Budo Technical Team. He started his karate journey in the year 2000 in the All stars Karate Shodan in 2002 and joined the Rivers State Karate team shortly after. Still an active athlete, he has competed in both kumite and karate events earning him gold, silver and bronze medals within the country and the West African region.

As the Technical Director, Sensei Bakare is responsible for implementing the Zen Budo training program. He believes in constant practice and in pushing beyond perceived limits. Always energetic and passionate about karate. Sensei Bakare is committed to impacting his knowledge to students at the Zen Budo Dojo.

Sensei Charles Adokiye Dagogo

Director School Karate

(4th Dan)

Sensei Charles Dagogo started his karate studies with the All Stars Shotokan Karate under the guidance of Mezie-Okoye Sensei in 2002. A versatile athlete, Sensei Charles had to make a mental shift from basketball and lean on his flexibility which he developed through his love for gymnastic. In 2008 he participated in the Rivers State Sports festival winning two gold medals and a silver medal in the process.

He obtained his Shodan rank in 2011 and his Sandan on 2016. At Zen Budo Karate, sensei Charles is saddled with the responsibility of growing our School Karate program.

Sensei Fredrick Ojehomon

Sports Director

(2nd Dan)

A true karate enthusiast, Sensei Fredrick Oare Ojehomon started his karate journey at the Zen Budo Karate dojo in 2018 as a white belt. A fervid karateka, his first few weeks in training quickly set him apart from his fellow students and set the foundation for his remarkable growth.

He was rewarded for his passion and dedication towards the art with his shodan in 2021.

Upon receiving his shadon, he joined the Zen Budo karate training team as an assistant instructor and successfully passed his nidan test under Shihan Mezie-Okoye in 2023. Sensei Fredrick serves as the sports Director on the Budo managenment Team.
