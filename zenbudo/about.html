About Us

We are a Marital Arts Academy committed to the promotion of peace and character building through the practice of traditional karate and meditaion.

At no other time has there been a strong call as there is today, for us to reawaken those traditional values, which have stood the test of time. And there i no better place to start this process than within ourselves. it is through knowledge of self that we can fully begin to manifest our four dimensions of intelligence (Body), Mental intelligence (Mind), Emotional intelligence (Heart) and Spiritual intelligence (Soul). We begin this process for you at Zen Budo Karate through our commitment to Self Defense, Fitness, Discipline and Meditation.

Vision

To be the service provider of choice for private individuals, schools, Corporate and Governmental Institutions in our market place.

Mission

To promote peace and enhance character building through the practice and teaching of traditional karate and meditation.
