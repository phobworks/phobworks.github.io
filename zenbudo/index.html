Karate Competition

Youth can be wasted on the young. While our focus is largely on the traditional aspects of karate, we take some time out at Zen Budo Karate to promote the sporting aspect of the art. This provides us with the opportunity to promote brotherhood among practitioners and provide an opportunity for karatekas to explore their sporting passion.

In partnership with our Corporate Sponsors, Strata Base Nigeria Limited, we have been able to organize karate events at the State, Regional and National levels. We also organise sponsorships for athletes to compete at international events.

Our Team

Sensei Amakiri Oruamabo

MD/Chief Instructor

(4th Dan)

Is a member of the Nigerian Karate Association (NKA) and the Zensyokan Karate Organizatio (Japan). He started his karate studies in 1988 under the tutelage of Mezie-Okoye Sensei, the founder of All-Stars Shotokan Karate Association. Having started karate at the young age, Sensei Oruamabo has a firsthand experience on the developmental impact of karate.

A licensed Zen Instructor, Sensei Oruamabo believes that karate isn't just about kicking and punching but a necessary tool through which we can develop and prepare the individual to approach life's challenges with unwavering self confidence and self belief.

As the MD/Chief Instructor, Sensei Oruamabo is responsible for both business development and developing a training program that is in line with the company's corporate vision.

Sensei Sensei Saidi Bakare

Technical Director

(4th Dan)

A versatile and accomplished karate-ka, Sensei Bakare heads the Zen Budo Technical Team. he started his karate journey in the year 2000 in the All Stars Karate Association under Mezie-Okoye Sensei. He earned his Shodan in 2002 and joined the Rivers State karate team shortly after. Still an active athlete, he has completed in bothe kumite and karate events earning him gold, silver & brom=nze medals within the country and the West African region.

As the Technical Director, Sensei Bakare is responsible for implementing the Zen Budo training program. He believes in constant practice and in pushing beyond perceived limits. Always energetic and passionate about karate, Sensei Bakare is committed to impacting his knowledge to students at the Zen Budo Dojo.

Sensei Charles Adokiye Dagogo

Director School Karate

(4th Dan)

Sensei Charles Dagogo started his karate studies with the All Stars Shotokan Karate under the guidence of Mezie-Okoye Sensei in 2002. A versatile athlete, Sensei Charles had to make a mental shift from basket ball and learn on his flexibiity which he developed through his love for gymnastics. In 2008 he participated in the Rivers State Sports festival winning two gold medals and a silver medal in the process.

He Obtained his shodan rank in 2011 and his Sandan on 2016. At Zen Budo Karate, Sensei Charles is saddled with the resposibility of growing our School Karate Program.

Sensei Fredrick Ojehomon

Sports Director

(2nd Dan)

A true karate enthusiast, Sensei Fredrick Oare Ojehomon started his karate journey at Zen Budo Karate Dojo in 2018 as a white belt. A fervid karate-ka, hsi few weeks in training quickly set him apart from his fellow students and set the foundation for his remarkable growth.

He was rewarded for his passion and dedication towards the art with his shodan in 2021

Upon receiving his shodan, he joined the Zen Budo karate training team as an assistant instructor and successfully passed hsi nidan test under Shihan Mezie-Okoye in 2023.

Sensei Fredrick serves as the Sports Director on the Zen Budo Management Team.
