About Us

Turandot Investments Limited is a leading cocoa exporting company based in Nigeria. We specialize in the production and export of high-quality cocoa, yam tuber, soya bean, oil palm products, and cashew nuts. Our mission is to provide premium cocoa products to the global market while supporting sustainable farming practices and improving the livelihoods of local farmers.

Sustainability and Ethical Practices

At Turandot Investments Limited, we are committed to sustainable farming practices and ethical sourcing. We work closely with local farmers to ensure fair prices and support their communities. Our initiatives include providing training on modern agricultural techniques, promoting the use of eco-friendly farming methods, and advocating against child labor in farm production.

Global Reach

Our mission is to establish strong trade relationships with major agro-importing countries. Our products are exported to various regions around the world, ensuring a reliable supply of high-quality farm products.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to become the leading farm product exporter in Africa, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical practices. Our mission is to provide premium farm products to the global market while supporting the development of local communities and promoting environmental sustainability.
