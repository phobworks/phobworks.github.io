About Us

Turandot Investments Limited is a leading cocoa exporting company based in Nigeria. We specialize in the production and export of high-quality cocoa beans, cocoa butter, cocoa powder, and other cocoa products.

Sustainability and Ethical Practices

At Turandot Investments Limited, we are committed to sustainable farming practices and ethical sourcing. We work closely with local farmers to ensure fair prices and support their communities. Our initiatives include providing training on modern agricultural techniques, promoting the use of eco-friendly farming methods, and advocating against child labor in cocoa production.

Global Reach

Our mission is to establish strong trade relationships with major cocoa-importing countries. Our products are exported to various regions around the world, ensuring a reliable supply of high-quality cocoa.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to become the leading cocoa exporter in Africa, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical practices. Our mission is to provide premium cocoa products to the global market while supporting the development of local communities and promoting environmental sustainability.

Our Commitment to Quality

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. From the farm to the final product, we ensure that every stage of production is carefully monitored.

  • Farm-Level Quality Checks: We collaborate with farmers to implement best practices in cocoa cultivation.

  • Advanced Processing Techniques: Our facilities use state-of-the-art technology to maintain the integrity of our cocoa products.

  • Stringent Testing Standards: Every batch undergoes rigorous quality testing to meet and exceed global standards.
