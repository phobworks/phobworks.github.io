About Us

Noble Care And Support

Noble Care and Support is a dedicated service provision company delivering personalized care and support to adults of all ages in their own homes, enabling them to maintain their independence and quality of life.

We believe that everyone deserves to live comfortably and safely and we are committed to providing compassionate and reliable care tailored to individual needs in the comfort of their own homes. We work in partnership with our service users to understand their specific needs and supply staff that are properly trained with a proven record of honesty and integrity.

Noble Care and Support has profound Quality Assurance policy and procedures in place and we are fully compliant, licensed and regulated by Care Quality Commission (CQC). We encourage you to take part in activities of your choice and support you to participate actively. Our aims and objectives are to promote independent flexible living. As our Service User, you will be in control of your future whilst we support with ranges of activities such as, from looking after your home, personal care or getting out into the community, attending appointments and socializing with people of similar interest.

Our carer will work with you to develop a care plan you're happy with.
