About

biography

Engr. Dr. Chukwudi Elekwachi Dimkpa was born on the 1st October.

He is an indigene of the ancient Kingdom of Isiokpo, the headquarters of the Ikwerre local Government Area of Rivers State, Nigeria.

He is married to Mrs. Marilyn Dimkpa and the married is blessed with three (3) Children.

His Family name "Dimkpa" means strong man in both Igbo and Ikwerre dialect.

Education

He received a Bachelor's in Electrical/Electronics Engineering, having graduated from the faculty of Engineering at the University of Port Harcourt, (UNIPORT). He proceeded to answer the clarion call of the National Youth Service Corps and he served at Shell Petroleum Development Company (SDPC) Warri in Delta State.

He also holds a Master Class Certificate in Business Administration from the London Graduate School as well as a Doctor of Philosophy degree in Business Administration (DBA).

He was appointed to the board of the Niger Delta Development Commission as commissioner representing Rivers State in the year 2023 under the administration of President Muhammadu Buhari

Engr. Dr. Chukwudi Dimkpa is a Change Management specialist with experience in managing change and business transformation. Restructuring organizations and establishing processes to meet both organizational needs

He is currently the Group Managing Director/CEO of Strata Base Group- comprising Strata Base (Nig.) Limited, Strata Base Properties Limited, ELCHUDIM Ventures Limited, Qoorier Limited, Estoric Media Limited and Quantum Marine Limited.

He is also the Chairman of the Board of UltraDeep South Energy Limited an Indigenous Oil and Gas Exploration and Production Company

His business interests cut across Real Estate, Building, Civil & Electrical Engineering, Construction, Logistics & Delivery, Marine, Oil & Gas and Out of Home Advertising. He has attended several trainings and courses in these fields

As the Pioneer Acting Managing Director of the Rivers State Signage and Advertisement Agency (RISAA). He was responsible for the establishment of the operational architecture that ensured effective and efficient take off/startup of operations of the Agency. He initiated and executed the first sensitization and enforcement of illegal and unauthorized billboards and outdoor structures in Rivers State, leading to improved outdoor landscape and civic beautification and improved revenue generation in the state. He introduced the automated payment system for transparent and accountable transactions, lead the team that represented the Agency in negotiation meetings with corporate organizations, outdoor structure owners/partners leading to the introduction of iconic and landmark structures in the state. He was also responsible for coordinating the deployment and posting of the various Out-of-Home concepts during the Rivers @50 anniversary celebrations with attendant event affinity and association leading to rave reviews and accolades from residents and visitors alike. He was also responsible for employment generation which saw many Rivers and nonRivers job seekers gainfully employed.

He is a Registered Engineer with the Council of Registered Engineers of Nigeria (COREN), Fellow, Nigeria Society of Engineers (FNSE), Fellow, Nigeria Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (FNIEEE), Associate Member, Nigerian Institution of Civil Engineers (AMNICE), Associate Member, Registered Practitioner in Advertising (arpa), Fellow, Institute of Corporate Administration (FCIA), Fellow, Institute of Management Consultants (FIMC), Member Institute of Electrical/ Electronics Engineering(MIEEE)

He is also a member of the Real Estate Developers Association of Nigeria (REDAN) - which he once headed the Rivers/Bayelsa States Chapter from 2004-2009 and a Member, South East/South-South Professionals of Nigeria (SESSPN).

He has a passion for football (and supports Arsenal FC), Karate, Travelling, Golf and plays Polo. He is an astute member of the Port Harcourt Polo Club of which he is a two-time Honorable Secretary of the club, two-time Vice President of the club and the immediate two-times President of the Port Harcourt Polo club. He is also a memeber of Port Harcourt club 1928. He is a Member of Guards club, Abuja. He a Memebr of Python golf club, Port Harcourt. He is the Patron of the Strata Base Polo Team and has won several laurels for his team and the club at large. He is a recipient of several awards including The Rotary International Award of Excellence in Community Service and has Honorary Membership of the Rotary Club of Port Harcourt Cosmopolitan Rotary International, District 9141, Nigeria. He is a Polio Ambassador of the Port Harcourt Highrise and a Paul Harris Fellow amongst others.

He is the Zonal Chairman, South- South and a member of the Board Karate Federation of Nigeria (KFN) representing the South South.

He is a past National President of his alma mater, University Demonstration Secondary School (UDSS), Uniport.

His Philanthropic works speaks volume as he is involved in community services bordering on free health care, youth empowerment, scholarships, mentorship and lots more.
