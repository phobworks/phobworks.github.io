Introduction

The strength of this test unit lies in its ability for on-site overhaul and testing of all common Shut-oﬀ Valves, Pressure Relief Valves and/or Control Valves. This on-site capability eliminates transportation of valves from site and workshop for repair as this can be done right there on site.

This test unit is very eﬀective during planned and unplanned shutdown with very limited start up time. It is very robust and works adequately under simultaneous operation (SIMOPS).
