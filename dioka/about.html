About

Dioka Integrated Services Limited was established in 2007 and registered on 1st July 2011 as a limited liability company. The company was established to enhance coverage of all the engineering needs of oil, non-oil, and gas companies (such as design/construction and maintenance of mechanical, civil, electrical, instrumentations, precurement, and general services).

Dioka Integrated Services Limited has gained vast and quality experience in Engineering and Construction. The company's scope of services is designed to the respective departments. The department' interest is to complement each other in technical abilities and so the actual services, which DIOKA INTEGRATED SERVICES LTDcan provide, are far greater than the sum of its constituent departments.
