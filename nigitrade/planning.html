Corporate Planning Strategy

In order to be at the head of the pack and give the very best to its clients, Nigitrade operates a planning and management system that is based on the latest Information Technology Platforms and Infrastructure.

The planning strategy encompasses the activities of planning proper, engineering, project management, designs, operations, documents control, materials management, corporate information management amongst others. This holistic management package makes for effective and well-informed scheduling of all Nigitrade's activities/operations on real time basis, thus placing the management in a vantage position to be able to effectively manage multiple contracts/operations through one facility.

To be able to achieve this, all Nigitrade offices, workshops, field stations, work-sites, operational areas and key personnel are all linked by a state-of-art information and telecommunication infrastructure.
