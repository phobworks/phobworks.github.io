who we are

Nigitrade: Shaping Dreams, Building Realities

Nigitrade International Company is an Indigenous Engineering, Procurement and Construction Company (EPC), provides specialized expertise in design, construction, procurement and installation in the efficient and sustainable management through its major operating divisions in Civil and Mechanical Engineering, Maritime Logistics and Human Resource Management. The holistic management package makes for effective and well-informed scheduling of all Nigitrade's activities/operations on real time basis.

what we do

The Company specializes in providing engineering services to assist clients in constructing and expanding onshore and offshore construction systems. Arrays of the services we provide include the following:

  • Integrated Marine Support Services

  • Complete Logistics and Operations Support Services

  • Human Resources Procurement, Training and Placement

  • Total Facilities Construction: New Builds, Upgrades, Operations and Maintenance.

  • Environmental management & protection and waste management services

  • Integrated Procurement, Services

  • Offshore logistics support services

  • Tank building and fabrication

  • Dredging, shoreline protection, flood and erosion control, Urban renewal schemes, Canalization works, River Flood sweeping and reclamation works

  • Project freight forwarding
