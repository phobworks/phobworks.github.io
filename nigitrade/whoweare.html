Who We Are

Nigitrade International Company is an Indigenous Engineering, Procurement and Construction Company (EPC), provides specialized expertise in design, construction, procurement and installation in the efficient and sustainable management through its major operating divisions in Civil and Mechanical Engineering, Maritime Logistics and Human Resource Management. The holistic management package makes for effective and well-informed scheduling of all Nigitrade's activities/operations on real time basis.

Nigitrade has established a strong, reliable and professional construction and installation company in the Niger Delta region of Nigeria. From utility buildings to industrial constructions we mobilize all resources required to fulfil client requirement.

Over the years we have executed projects and offered services in engineering, construction, procurement, civil/mechanical works, industrial installation and development.

Our impressive list of achievements lend credence to being employed by numerous multiÂ­ national clients in offshore and onshore engineering projects all of which have been executed professionally to clients requirement.

The vast experience gained in completing many complex projects around the Niger Delta coastal region places us on a pedestal of speciality in highly complex engineering, procurement and construction for clients in remote and challenging locations. Nigitrade has successfully completed projects in the areas of power and industrial construction, downstream and upstream infrastructure in oil and gas, construction engineering and Industrial fabrication associated with onshore, coastal and offshore locations.

We are also actively involved in asset development, ownership and operations as an extension of our construction and engineering services.

Nigitrade has worked in collaboration with a number of International Oil & Gas Services Companies and has subsisting JOint Venture Agreement OVA) with BENTINI SPAand PARESA SPA two frontline Italian EPC companies with a track record of brilliant project deliveries across the globe as well aswith several Indigenous corporations in the construction and oil and gas industry.

Nigitrade has adequately intensified its EPC capability internally as well as through acquisition and incorporation and respectably competes in EPC for Oil, Gas and Energy projects for specialized civil, marine, mechanical and electrical designs, engineering and construction projects.
