Tama Nisa The Artist

Where words fail, music speaks, to soothe the mind and calm the heart, with its rhythm and flow, your soul made whole.

Born and raised in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, I am the typical essence of the African middle class young woman. I enjoy travel, fashion as a form of art and personal expression. I am Tama Nisa and music is my life, my art is an expression of our African Royal Heritage, a reminder of Love, Romance, elevation of the mind, and call to create a strong, united and thriving Africa. I am a dreamer.

The EP- Pillars and Leaves is a collection of diverse African beats and rhythm to create a fused style of sensual Afro pop, Afro house, and Afro soul that tells about our passion, resilience, strength, love, romance, and the essence that drives humanity...

The Other Life of Tama Nisa

On this journey called life, we are all tailored to our unique experiences that shapes us and makes us. Sometimes something new is born for the greatest good of humanity, other times it brings about chaos and self-destruction.

Our world today, we see the echoes of negativity around us and the need for more voices of hope and the courage to persevere cannot be over emphasized.

That is why I lend my voice not only through my music but activating programs and building the brand to become an icon of hope, re-invention of self, inspire ideas for positive influences advocating mental well- being by creating a support system for people struggling with depression.
