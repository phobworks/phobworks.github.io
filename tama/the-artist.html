About The Artist

Where words fail, music speaks, to soothe the mind and calm the heart, with its rhythm and flow, your soul made whole.

Born and raised in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, I am the typical essence of the African middle class young woman. I enjoy travel, fashion as a form of art and personal expression. I am Tama Nisa and music is my life, my art is an expression of our African Royal Heritage, a reminder of Love, Romance, elevation of the mind, and call to create a strong, united and thriving Africa. I am a dreamer.

The EP- Pillars and Leaves is a collection of diverse African beats and rhythm to create a fused style of sensual Afro pop, Afro house, and Afro soul that tells about our passion, resilience, strength, love, romance, and the essence that drives humanity

Tama Nisa brings a wealth of experience to bear demonstrating her vocal and artistic prowess on platforms, like Nigerian idols, the voice and her life changing arts project for young people young gifted artist Nigeria (YGAN) her desire to encourage young people to pursue their passion by living their dreams through the arts has endeared her to many.

With four hit singles to her credit plans are ongoing for massive promotions of Tama's work throughout the continent, as part of her growth structure Tama Nisa seeks to form a formidable band of international standard, a studio to record and produce world class music and an international music events to celebrate our local talent and musical heritage.

Musician

Tama Nisa's musical prowess transcends genres, blending soulful melodies with Afrobeat rhythms, creating a sound that resonates globally.
