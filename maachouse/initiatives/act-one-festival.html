Act One Festival

Introducing Act One Festival

The Act One Festival, powered by MaaC House Ltd., is a transformative cultural and social impact initiative set to take place in December 2025. This uniquely curated festival is a vibrant celebration of the Ijaw heritage, designed to spotlight the richness of our culture, empower human potential, and foster progressive societal change.

Rooted in the themes; Achievement, Poly-Poly, and Boys to Kings, the festival goes beyond mere entertainment. It is a platform for youth empowerment, family healing, and leadership cultivation, aiming to inspire pride In our unique identity, promote excellence, and ignite a legacy of transformation within the community.

Act One Festival unfolds as a year-long journey of empowerment, culminating in an unforgettable December celebration. Through skill acquisition programs, mentorship, thought-provoking seminars, and awe-inspiring cultural showcases, it weaves a narrative of progress and unity. We welcome you to create with us a historic experience that bridges the gap between culture and modernity, inspiring the next generation of achievers, healers, and leaders. The Act One Festival is where the past, present, and future meet to create a legacy of excellence.

Our Mission

To showcase Ijaw excellence, empower youths, heal societal divides, and nurture leaders for a better tomorrow.

Our Vision

To create a legacy-driven festival that inspires pride in Ijaw culture, nurtures talent, builds stronger, united and thriving communities.

Festival Key Themes

Achievement Theme

  • Empowerment Academy (Sept-Nov 2025): Skills training in arts, technology, sports, entrepreneurship, personal development and positive transformational program.
  • Showcases at the Festival: Celebrating the brilliance of youths with unique performances and exhibitions.
  • Awards Night: Honoring outstanding achievers in the empowerment academy, creative and professional fields.

Poly-Poly Theme

  • Workshops and Seminars (Nov-Dec 2025): Educating on ethical polygamy, healing family and societal rifts.
  • Storytelling and Community Panels: Highlighting positive examples of family harmony.

Boys to Kings Theme

  • Mentorship Programs (Sept-Dec 2025): Pairing young men with successful role models.
  • Workshops: Covering financial literacy, emotional intelligence, and self-discipline.
  • Boys to Kings Summit: Culmination of training with keynote speeches, debates, and leadership awards.

Why Act One Festival?

Cultural Significance

Act, The Heartbeat of SAGBAMA, boasts a rich cultural heritage that deserves a global stage.

Economic Impact

The festival aims to boost local economies by attracting tourism, promoting local businesses, and creating job opportunities.

Youth Empowerment

Providing a platform for young creatives to showcase talent, network, and build sustainable careers.

Bridging Generations
