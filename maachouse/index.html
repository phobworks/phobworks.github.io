Our Core Initiatives

MaaC House is deeply rooted in initiatives that bring life to our vision of fostering a vibrant arts and culture ecosystem. Each program is carefully designed to inspire, educate, and create opportunities for talents to rise and thrive.

The MaaC House Incubator Program

Creativity needs nurturing, and that's where the MaaC House Incubator Program comes in. This initiative provides an immersive, hands-on environment for emerging talents in the arts and cultural industries. Participants receive mentorship from seasoned professionals, access to industry resources, and a collaborative space to reﬁne their craft. Whether you're a budding musician, writer, ﬁlmmaker, or entrepreneur, the MaaC House Incubator Program is designed to empower you with the tools to succeed.

Act One Festival

The Art One Festival is a groundbreaking initiative designed to celebrate the richness of the African culture while fostering human capacity development and social impact. With themes rooted in empowerment, ethical family structures, and leadership, the festival promises to be a transformative event for the community and other African communities.
