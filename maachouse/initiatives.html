Initiatives

Our Core Initiatives

MaaC House is deeply rooted in initiatives that bring life to our vision of fostering a vibrant arts and culture ecosystem. Each program is carefully designed to inspire, educate, and create opportunities for talents to rise and thrive.

The MaaC House Incubator Program

Creativity needs nurturing, and that’s where the MaaC House Incubator Program comes in. This initiative provides an immersive, hands-on environment for emerging talents in the arts and cultural industries. Participants receive mentorship from seasoned professionals, access to industry resources, and a collaborative space to reﬁne their craft. Whether you’re a budding musician, writer, ﬁlmmaker, or entrepreneur, the MaaC House Incubator Program is designed to empower you with the tools to succeed.

Goals

  • Foster creativity and innovation in the arts.
  • Equip participants with industry-relevant skills and knowledge.
  • Build a supportive network for cultural and artistic growth.

Benefits

  • Access to expert mentorship from seasoned professionals.
  • Hands-on workshops and training sessions.
  • Collaborative spaces to refine and showcase creative works.
  • Opportunities for networking and industry exposure.

Agbere One Festival

The Agbere One Festival is a groundbreaking initiative designed to celebrate the richness of the Ijaw culture while fostering human capacity development and social impact. With themes rooted in empowerment, ethical family structures, and leadership, the festival promises to be a transformative event for the community and other African communities.

Themes

Empowerment, ethical family structures, and leadership, fostering a shared vision for cultural growth and community development.

Impact

The festival brings communities together, promotes cultural pride, and drives positive social and economic change, leaving a lasting legacy of inspiration and empowerment.

Sustainable Mentorship Model

We believe in the power of mentorship to create lasting change. MaaC House's Sustainable Mentorship Model pairs aspiring creatives with experienced mentors across various disciplines. This program fosters intergenerational collaboration, ensures knowledge transfer, and creates a support system for mentees to navigate their careers with conﬁdence.

Mentorship Pairings

Our mentorship program connects aspiring creatives with seasoned professionals in their fields, fostering intergenerational collaboration. Each pairing is carefully curated to ensure alignment in goals, skills, and growth opportunities, providing mentees with personalized guidance.

Success Stories

From mentees launching successful businesses to mentors finding renewed purpose, our program has transformed countless lives. One mentee shared, “My mentor's support helped me turn my passion into a thriving career,” while another mentor noted, “The experience of shaping young talent has been incredibly rewarding.”
