Mission

At Steve-Charis Nig Ltd, our mission is to be a premier provider of comprehensive and innovative solutions in the oil and gas servicing industry, and the Agro-allied industry too. Fueled by a commitment to excellence, safety, and sustainability, we strive to exceed client expectations by delivering cutting-edge technologies and services. We aim to be a strategic partner, facilitating the success of our clients while contributing positively to the energy sector. Our relentless pursuit of operational efficiency, environmental stewardship, and unwavering integrity defines us as a trusted ally in the dynamic and evolving landscape of the oil and gas industry

Vision

To be a global leader in the oil and gas servicing industry setting unparalleled standards of excellence through innovation, sustainability, and unwavering commitment. We envision a future where Steve-Charis Nig Ltd is synonymous with trail blazing solutions, responsible resource management, and enduring partnerships. By consistently exceeding expectations, embracing technological advancements, and fostering a culture of integrity, we aspire to drive positive change in the energy sector while ensuring the long-term success of our clients and stakeholders

Goal

Our goal at Steve-Charis Nig Ltd is to be a catalyst for progress in the oil and gas sector, Agro-allied industry and civil work services. We aspire to consistently deliver unparalleled value to our clients by providing innovative, reliable, and sustainable solutions. Through a relentless pursuit of operational excellence, technological advancement, and a commitment to environmental responsibility, we aim to set new industry standards. Our focus on fostering long-term partnerships, promoting safety, and embracing adaptability positions us to navigate the challenges of the energy landscape.
