About
Dappa Solomon

Visionary Photographer & Filmmaker

Welcome to the captivating world of DAPPA Solomon, a multifaceted artist whose lens captures stories, emotions, and the essence of life.

Education and Certification

Dappa Solomon is a certified digital lifestyle designer, having graduated from the Information Technology Academy (ITA). His academic journey also led him to attain a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the prestigious Rivers State University of Science and Technology, now Rivers State University.

Creative Ventures

As a member of the Cinematographer's Society of Nigeria, Dappa Solomon is not just a skilled photographer but also the Founder and Creative Director of I-Pac Pictures, a platform where creativity, storytelling, and visual mastery converge.

International Exposure and Mentorship

Driven by an insatiable thirst for knowledge, Dappa Solomon has ventured beyond the borders of Nigeria to hone his craft. He has been mentored by esteemed filmmakers, including the renowned Mr. Haile Gerima from Ethiopia and Mark Brower from South Africa, enriching his understanding of the global cinematic landscape.

A Journey into Photography

Dappa's journey into photography traces back to his secondary school days when he collaborated with his aunty, Pastor Mrs. Girlie Siminialayi, capturing moments of her decoration jobs. The year 2008 marked the genesis of his passion, a flame that has only intensified over the years.

A Trailblazer in Exhibitions

Notably, Dappa Solomon made history as the youngest photo exhibitor at the Niger Delta Phototales, standing alongside esteemed photographers such as Sir George Osodi, Mr. Bayo Omoboriowo, Sir. M.M Abasa, and Timi Willis Amah. His early participation in prestigious events like Luxor Africa International Film Festival (LAFF) and Africa International Film Festival (AFRIFF) underscores his commitment to continuous learning.

Vision and Aspirations

Dappa Solomon envisions himself as a renowned, internationally recognized photojournalist and a household name in the photography industry. His mission is to see his works celebrated globally, displayed in notable museums for exhibitions, echoing the legacy of pioneers like Adagogo Green.

Photography: A Chronicle of Time

For Dappa Solomon, photography is a profound act of capturing moments that would otherwise be lost. It is the preservation of memories for generations unborn, a testament to the impermanence of time.

Role Models and Inspirations

Among his role models is the esteemed Sir George Osodi, whose documentary styles have left an indelible mark on Dappa Solomon's artistic journey. A one-on-one encounter with Sir George Osodi further solidified the admiration for his mentor's impactful contributions to the world of photography.

Join Dappa Solomon on his odyssey through the visual realm, where every frame tells a story, and every click freezes a moment in time. Embrace the journey, and let the images speak for themselves.
