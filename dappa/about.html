About
Dappa Solomon

Visionary Photographer & Story Teller

Welcome to the captivating world of Dappa Solomon, a multifaceted artist whose lens captures stories, emotions, and the essence of life.

Dappa is a certified digital lifestyle designer and holds a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Rivers State University of Science and Technology. As the Founder and Creative Director of I-Pac Pictures, he merges creativity, storytelling, and visual mastery.

Mentored by esteemed filmmakers like Haile Gerima and Mark Brower, Dappa's global exposure enriches his cinematic understanding. His journey into photography began in secondary school, collaborating with family to capture moments.

Notably, Dappa made history as the youngest exhibitor at the Niger Delta Phototales, standing alongside esteemed photographers like Sir George Osodi, Mr. Bayo Omoboriowo, Sir. M.M Abasa, Timi Willis Amah, and others.

With a vision to become a renowned photojournalist, Dappa's mission is to see his works celebrated globally, preserving memories for generations. Inspired by Sir George Osodi, Dappa's photography chronicles time, freezing moments that tell profound stories.

Some notable international brands Dappa Solomon has worked with include Standard Chartered Bank, Valco Construction Company Ltd, George & Assorts, NLNG, etc.

Join Dappa Solomon on his odyssey through the visual realm, where every frame narrates a tale, and every click immortalizes a moment. Let the images speak for themselves.
