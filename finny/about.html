About Us

FINNY INTEGRATED SERVICES LIMITED (FINTSERV) is a company established under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990 and incorporated in 2010, to meet all training needs and management systems requirements for both Oil & gas and Non-oil & Gas sectors, especially in the area of safety technical studies, logistics, procurement, training & manpower development, supply chain services and construction.

For this purpose, FINTSERV can guarantee a number of dedicated, highly skilled personnel capable of providing quality services to meet or exceed clients requirement.

FINTSERV as a company has built a strong team with vast experience that can facilitate service delivery.

FINTSERV has a pool of experts who are called on a need-basis to carry out trainings, render management systems consultancy services for her clients

Our Mision

The mission of Finny Integrated services is to provide clients with a quality and complete solution to their Health and Safety and environmental needs both in training, safety equipment supplies and management system support services. With our exceptional functional and technical expertise coupled with extensive industry knowledge, great taste for quality and professional partnerships, we sure leave our clients more than satisfied with our services. This makes FINTSERV, the ideal choice for partnership.
