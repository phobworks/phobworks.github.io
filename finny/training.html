Training

Training Synopses

Work at Hieght Training

The Working at Height Training Course is designed for individuals whose job responsibilities involve working at elevated heights. This course aims to educate participants on the risks of working at heights and how to implement safety practices to prevent accidents and injuries

Confined Space Entery Training

This course fulfills the basic requirements for employees who may be exposed to hazardous conditions present in Confined Space work environment. Participants will gain a greater understanding of hazards specific to Confined Spaces. They will gain comprehensive knowledge and acquire tools to eliminate the hazards associated with working in confined spaces so as to bring the associated risks to the lowest level.

Hygrogen Sulfide(H2S) Training

This course is designed to provide participants of this course with the necessary information about hydrogen sulphide (H2S). It is a course that explains to workers who are likely to be exposed to H2S at work the properties of H2S, identifies control measures and provides recommendations of what to do in the event of exposure.

Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) Training

This SCBA training has been tailored to provide users of the personal protective equipment the information required to use and maintain the equipment. It provides information on how to properly don it, and proper use of it. This is a hands-on course on the use of the using the equipment which will also provide techniques needed in an emergency response situation while working in a confined space that has low oxygen availability with the presence of other harmful gases.

Basic Scaffold Training

This course will give learners the awareness of legislative requirements, the knowledge of components of a scaffold, the purpose they serve in ensuring its stability and the requirements of a scaffold inspection. It would expose learners to the structure of scaffold which is created on temporary basis to facilitate the erection, construction, repair & maintenance of scaffold when work is to be carried out at the height.
